WOODEN TOYS

Wooden toys are one of the oldest in history. They are classic but not less interesting. These toys with their rustic design, curious shapes and cute light and dark wood colors delighted children who spent hours playing using their imagination to create stories and adventures with these toys that could be shaped like a duck with wheels or a simple cart. Also, many of these toys served for the basic education of children such as cubes with letters, puzzles, etc.
